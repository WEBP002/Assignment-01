Restaurant Policies

1. Reservation Policy
• Reservations are highly recommended to ensure availability, especially during peak hours.
• To make a reservation, customers must provide their name, phone number, email address, date and time of the reservation, and the number of guests.
• We will hold reservations for 15 minutes after the scheduled time, after which the reservation will be considered a no-show.
• We reserve the right to release unclaimed reservations to waiting customers after 15 minutes.
2. Cancellation Policy
• If you need to cancel your reservation, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.
• To cancel a reservation, please call us or cancel online through our website or reservation system
• If you cancel less than 24 hours in advance, or if you fail to show up for your reservation, we reserve the right to charge a cancellation fee of $25 per person.
• If you have made a deposit to secure your reservation, it is non-refundable if you cancel less than 24 hours in advance or if you fail to show up for your reservation.
3. Deposit Policy
• For parties of 6 or more, a deposit of $50 per person may be required to secure the reservation.
• Deposits must be paid in advance by credit card or cash.
• The deposit will be applied towards the final bill on the day of the reservation.
• Deposits are non-refundable if you cancel less than 24 hours in advance or if you fail to show up for your reservation.
