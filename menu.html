Introduction section

Chef John Smith opened his first restaurant, "Smith's Kitchen," in 1998 and quickly gained popularity for his delicious dishes and welcoming atmosphere. He expanded his menu to include international flavors and opened a second, more upscale restaurant called "Smith's Table." The new restaurant earned numerous awards and became one of the city's most sought-after reservations. Chef John Smith continued to innovate and evolve his menu while maintaining a commitment to sustainability. Today, he is a culinary icon in the city, and his restaurants are beloved by locals and visitors alike.

Roasted root vegetables with rosemary (winter)

- Spanish: Verduras de raíz asadas con romero (invierno)
- French: Légumes racines rôtis au romarin (hiver)
- Mandarin: 过冬烤远古蔬菜与迷迭香
description
This hearty winter dish features a medley of roasted root vegetables with a touch of rosemary. The vegetables are tender and caramelized, with a subtle earthy flavor that pairs perfectly with the aromatic rosemary.
ingredients
sweet potatoes, parsnips, carrots, onion, garlic, olive oil, rosemary
preparation method
Preheat, chop, Season, Roast
